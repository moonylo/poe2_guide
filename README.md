# PoE 2: Ice Shot / Snipe Deadeye Leveling Guide 
*Based on Fubgun's Level 1 to Maps Walkthrough*

**Important Note:** You do not actually unlock the "Ice Shot" skill until Level 31. Therefore, the early game relies heavily on **Lightning Arrow** and **Lightning Rod**. 

---

## Part 1: Act 1 (Levels 1 - 16)
Act 1 is notoriously the hardest part of the campaign for bow builds. Following these specific steps will make it much smoother.

### Boss & Zone Order (Highly Recommended)
Unless you are a hardcore speedrunner, follow this exact path to get vital support gems:
1.  **Devourer** (Mud Barrow): Grants a Support Gem (20% more damage for the next few bosses).
2.  **Cold Witch** (Clearfell): Grants 10% Cold Resistance.
3.  **Hut Witch** (Gravelwood): Grants a Support Gem.
4.  **Rust King** (The Red Vale).

### Early Gear Priority
*   **FLAT DAMAGE IS KING:** The difference between having flat damage and not having it in Act 1 is literally double your total DPS. Look for **Flat Physical** or **Flat Elemental Damage to Attacks** on:
    *   Gloves
    *   Rings
    *   Quiver
    *   Bow
    *(Use Orbs of Transmutation/Augmentation on rings/quivers to hit these stats).*
*   **Boots:** Always look for Movement Speed.
*   **Defenses:** Grab Life and Resistances wherever you can on Helmets, Chests, and Belts.
*   **Attributes:** You will need Strength and Intelligence for early gems. Look for a Heavy Belt (Str) or an Amulet with Attributes. 

### Bow Progression (Act 1)
*   **Level 5 (Shortbow):** Farmed in *The Red Vale*. Look for "Refined Arms" weapon racks. 
    *   *Pro-Tip:* If you find the checkpoint, you can click a weapon rack, hit ESC -> "Resurrect at Checkpoint" to reset the zone, and click it again until a Shortbow drops. Use Transmute/Augment Orbs on it.
*   **Level 11 (Warden Bow):** Grants a chance to chain (great for Lightning Rod). Do not invest heavy currency here; just use Transmutes/Augments. 
*   **Level 16 (Recurve Bow):** This is your first major upgrade. **Save your Orbs of Alchemy, Exalted Orbs, Runes, and Sockets for this bow.** It will carry you deep into Act 2. 

### Act 1 Skill Setup & Boss Rotation
*   **Main Skills:** Lightning Arrow, Lightning Rod.
*   **Supports to use:** Rapid Attacks, Elemental Armaments I, Concentrated Area. 
*   **Bossing Combo:** 
    1. Place 4x Lightning Rods on the ground.
    2. Drop a Frost Bomb (Keep Frost bomb at **Level 3 maximum** so it can affect lower-level enemies).
    3. Stand still (if safe) and spam Lightning Arrow into the Rods. 
*   **Level 14 Addition:** You unlock *Ice-Tipped Arrows* and *Barrage*. Use Barrage on cooldown to instantly fire 3 fast shots into your Lightning Rods for burst damage. Ice-Tipped arrows add passive bonus damage.
*   **Mobility (Optional):** Equip a Wolf Talisman and slot **Pounce** (Level 3). Transforming into a wolf gives massive movement speed and leap distance.

## Part 2: Act 2 & The Level 24 "Big Swap"
This is where the build transforms and starts dealing massive single-target damage.

### The Swap (Level 24/25)
Drop Lightning Rod. You are now transitioning to a **Snipe** and **Freezing** setup. 

*   **You need a 4-Link for Snipe:** Farm the *Buried Shrines* zone (Level 24/25). Completing the league mechanic in this zone guarantees a **Lesser Jeweller's Orb**, allowing you to 4-link your Snipe.
*   **Snipe Links:** Snipe + Window of Opportunity + Concentrated Area + Elemental Armaments II. *(Note: If Window of Opportunity feels too clunky, use Elemental Focus instead).*

### The "Boss Freeze & Snipe" Rotation
To deal massive damage, you must freeze the boss first. Practice this rotation:
1.  **Pre-cast:** Ice-Tipped Arrows.
2.  **Mark:** Cast *Freezing Mark* on the boss.
3.  **Build Freeze:** Shoot *Lightning Arrow* 1 to 3 times.
4.  **Trigger Freeze:** Cast *Freezing Salvo*. (The boss should now be frozen).
5.  **Nuke:** Press *Barrage*, then immediately charge up **Snipe**. 
    *   *How to Snipe:* Hold the button until your character glows bright green, then release for massive damage.

### General Clear Rotation
1. Keep *Ice-Tipped Arrows* active.
2. Cast *Freezing Mark* on a target (grants 30% bonus cold damage when enemies are frozen).
3. Spam *Lightning Arrow*.

## Part 3: Act 3, Ice Shot, & Weapon Swapping (Level 31+)
At Level 31, the build officially becomes the "Ice Shot" build, and you unlock a massively powerful passive tree mechanic.

### Skill Changes
*   Swap Lightning Arrow for **Ice Shot**.
*   Swap Herald of Thunder for **Herald of Ice**.

### The Weapon Swap Mechanic (Crucial for Damage)
In PoE 2, you can allocate specific passive tree points to specific weapon sets. We abuse this to get massive Mark buffs without losing DPS.

1.  **Setup:** Equip a Bow and Quiver in Weapon Set 1. Right-click both and select **"Lock to all weapon sets."** (Your offhand will now use the exact same weapons).
2.  **Passive Tree - Weapon Set 1 (Damage):** Assign ALL of your damage nodes (Projectile damage, attack speed, cold damage) to Weapon Set 1. 
3.  **Passive Tree - Weapon Set 2 (Marks):** Assign the **10 Mark nodes** on the tree (Mark Effect, Mark Cast Speed, Mark Duration, Enemies take increased damage) strictly to Weapon Set 2. 
4.  **Skill Gem Assignment:** 
    *   Assign **ONLY Freezing Mark** to Weapon Set 2.
    *   Assign **ALL OTHER SKILLS** (Ice Shot, Snipe, Auras, Spirit Gems) to Weapon Set 1.
5.  **How it works in combat:** The game auto-swaps to Set 2 when you cast Freezing Mark (granting you massive freeze buildup, 20-second mark duration, and faster cast speed), and instantly auto-swaps back to Set 1 when you attack with Ice Shot/Snipe to deal maximum damage.

### Bow Upgrades (Level 33 to Maps)
*   **Level 33 (Cultist Bow / Dualstring Bow):** Start checking town vendors (Zarka/Shambrin/Risu the Gambler) for these bases. 
    *   You are looking for **High % Physical Damage**. % Physical is the best stat you can get.
    *   *Note:* If using a Dualstring Bow (which requires level 28), make sure the **Item Level** (hold ALT) is at least 33 so it can roll the higher-tier damage affixes.
    *   Once you find a good base: Use Alchemy Orbs, Regals, Exalted Orbs, Whetstones (20% quality), and socket your Cold/Lightning Runes into it. This bow will carry you through Act 3.
*   **Level 46 (Artillery Bow):** Found in Act 4. Follow the exact same crafting process as above. A well-crafted Artillery Bow will carry you all the way into the endgame/mapping.

## Quick Reference Checklists

**Flasks:**
Constantly upgrade your life flasks. The upgrade milestones are levels **10, 16, 23, and 30**. Always buy the highest tier from the vendor before major boss fights.

**Passive Tree / Ascendancy Priority:**
1. Point Blank (Proximity Specialization)
2. Endless Munitions
3. Gathering Winds

**Gem Leveling Priority:**
*   **Snipe:** Keep this at the absolute highest level possible. It is your primary nuke.
*   **Ice Shot / Lightning Arrow:** Secondary priority for clearing. 
*   **Freezing Mark:** Try to get a high-quality version of this gem later on (adds 20% more freeze buildup).
