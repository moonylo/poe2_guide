# PoE 2: Ice Shot / Snipe Deadeye Leveling Guide 
*Complete Level 1 to Maps Walkthrough*

## ⚠️ Crucial Campaign Rules (Read Before Starting)

**1. Uncut Gem & Currency Management (DO NOT BRICK YOUR BUILD)**
*   **Hoard High-Level Gems:** DO NOT use any Level 6 or Level 7 Uncut Skill/Support Gems early. Save them all in your stash. You absolutely need them to unlock your Snipe setup at Level 24 and your Ice Shot setup at Level 31.
*   **Lesser Jeweller's Orbs:** Save your **first** Lesser Jeweller's Orb exclusively for **Snipe** (Level 24). Save your **second** Lesser Jeweller's Orb exclusively for **Ice Shot** (Level 31).

**2. The Vendor Regex & Shopping**
*   Town vendor inventories **reset every single time you level up**. Check them every 1-2 levels for Rings, Amulets, and Belts.
*   Copy and paste this exact Regex into the vendor search bar to highlight items with Flat Damage or Bows: 
    `"nne|nne"|l p|"d a"|n bow|`

**3. General Gearing & Tree Priorities**
*   **Flat Damage is King in Act 1/2:** Flat Physical or Elemental Damage to Attacks on Rings, Gloves, Quivers, and Bows will literally double your DPS early on.
*   **Armor Bases:** Evasion or Evasion/Energy Shield bases are preferred, but **Stats > Base Type**. A pure Armor piece with +Life and +Resistances is better than an Evasion piece with nothing.
*   **Attributes:** You will lack Strength and Intelligence early. **Take +30 STR or INT nodes on the passive tree** to wear your gear. You can easily respec them at the vendor later once your amulets/belts fix your stats.
*   **Flasks:** Upgrade your life flasks at levels **10, 16, 23, and 30**. Always buy the highest tier from the vendor before major boss fights.

## Act 1: Levels 1 - 16

### Boss & Zone Order (Highly Recommended)
Unless you are speedrunning, follow this exact path to get vital early support gems:
1.  **Devourer** (Mud Barrow): Grants a Support Gem (20% more damage for early bosses).
2.  **Cold Witch** (Clearfell): Grants 10% Cold Resistance.
3.  **Hut Witch** (Gravelwood): Grants a Support Gem.
4.  **Rust King** (The Red Vale).

### Bow Progression
*   **Level 5 (Shortbow):** Farmed in *The Red Vale*. Look for "Refined Arms" weapon racks. 
    *   *Pro-Tip:* If you find the checkpoint, click a weapon rack, hit ESC -> "Resurrect at Checkpoint" to reset the zone, and click it again until a Shortbow drops. Use Transmute/Augment Orbs on it.
*   **Level 11 (Warden Bow):** Grants a chance to chain (great for Lightning Rod). Do not invest heavy currency here; just use Transmutes/Augments. 
*   **Level 16 (Recurve Bow):** This is your first major upgrade. **Save your Orbs of Alchemy, Exalted Orbs, Runes, and Sockets for this bow.** It will carry you deep into Act 2. 

### Skill Setup & Boss Rotation
*   **Main Skills:** Lightning Arrow, Lightning Rod.
*   **Supports to use:** Rapid Attacks, Elemental Armaments I, Concentrated Area. 
*   **Frost Bomb (Level 3 MAX):** Keep Frost Bomb at **Level 3 maximum**. In PoE 2, utility gems only apply to monsters equal to or below the gem's requirement. A Lvl 3 Frost Bomb works up to Level 25 monsters. After that, it stops working entirely (which is when we drop it).
*   **Level 14 Addition:** You unlock *Ice-Tipped Arrows* and *Barrage*. Use Barrage on cooldown to instantly fire 3 fast shots into your Lightning Rods for burst damage.
*   **Bossing Combo:** Place 4x Lightning Rods -> Drop Frost Bomb -> Stand still and spam Lightning Arrow into the Rods. 
*   **Mobility (Optional):** Equip a Wolf Talisman and slot **Pounce** (Level 3) for massive movement speed and leap distance.

## Act 2: The "Snipe Swap" (Levels 16 - 30)

At Level 24/25, your build transforms to deal massive single-target damage. You will drop Lightning Rod and Frost Bomb.

### The Swap Setup
1.  **Get your 4-Link Snipe:** Farm the *Buried Shrines* zone (Level 24/25). Completing the league mechanic here guarantees a **Lesser Jeweller's Orb**. Use it to 4-link your Snipe.
2.  **Snipe Links:** Snipe + Window of Opportunity + Concentrated Area + Elemental Armaments II. *(If Window of Opportunity feels too clunky, use Elemental Focus instead).*

### The "Boss Freeze & Snipe" Rotation
To deal massive damage, you must freeze the boss first.
1.  **Pre-cast:** Ice-Tipped Arrows.
2.  **Mark:** Cast *Freezing Mark* on the boss (grants 30% bonus cold damage to frozen enemies).
3.  **Build Freeze:** Shoot *Lightning Arrow* 1 to 3 times.
4.  **Trigger Freeze:** Cast *Freezing Salvo*. The boss should now be frozen.
5.  **Nuke:** Press *Barrage*, then immediately charge up **Snipe** until you glow bright green, and release.

**⚠️ SNIPE TIMING WARNING:** If you take too long to charge your Snipe, the boss will unfreeze before the hit lands. If you see the boss unfreeze while charging, **do not release Snipe.** Instead, cast *Freezing Salvo* (or Escape Shot) to re-freeze them, *then* release Snipe.

*(General Clear Rotation: Keep Ice-Tipped Arrows active -> Cast Freezing Mark on tanky targets -> Spam Lightning Arrow).*

## Act 3: The "Ice Shot" & Weapon Swap (Levels 31 - 40)

At Level 31, you unlock Ice Shot and a massively powerful passive tree mechanic. 

### Skill Changes
*   Swap Lightning Arrow for **Ice Shot**. (Use your 2nd Lesser Jeweller's Orb here).
*   Swap Herald of Thunder for **Herald of Ice**.
*   **Uncut Gem Leveling Priority from here on:** 1. Snipe -> 2. Ice Shot -> 3. Freezing Mark -> 4. Heralds.

### The Weapon Swap Mechanic (CRITICAL FOR DPS)
We abuse PoE 2's weapon set skill tree to get massive Mark buffs without losing damage.

1.  **Gear Setup:** Equip your Bow and Quiver in Weapon Set 1. Right-click both and select **"Lock to all weapon sets."** (You now use the same bow in both slots).
2.  **Passive Tree (Set 1 - Damage):** Assign ALL of your damage nodes (Projectile damage, attack speed, cold damage) strictly to Weapon Set 1. 
3.  **Passive Tree (Set 2 - Marks):** Assign the **10 Mark nodes** on the tree (Mark Effect, Mark Cast Speed, Mark Duration, Enemies take increased damage) strictly to Weapon Set 2. 
4.  **Skill Gem Assignment:** 
    *   Assign **ONLY Freezing Mark** to Weapon Set 2.
    *   Assign **ALL OTHER SKILLS** (Ice Shot, Snipe, Auras, Spirit Gems) to Weapon Set 1.
*Result: When you cast Freezing Mark, the game auto-swaps to Set 2 (granting massive freeze buildup, 20s mark duration, and cast speed), and instantly auto-swaps back to Set 1 when you attack with Ice Shot/Snipe to deal maximum damage.*

### Level 33 Bow Upgrade
*   **Bases to look for:** Cultist Bow or Dualstring Bow.
*   **Requirement:** Make sure the **Item Level** (hold ALT) is at least **33** so it can roll the higher-tier damage affixes.
*   **Crafting:** You are looking for **High % Physical Damage**. % Physical is the best stat you can get. Use Alchemy Orbs, Regals, Exalted Orbs, Whetstones (20% quality), and socket your Cold/Lightning Runes into it. This bow will carry you into Act 4.

## Act 4 to Endgame (Levels 41+)

*   **Final Campaign Bow (Level 46):** Look for an **Artillery Bow** (Item Level 46+). Follow the exact same crafting process as the Level 33 bow. A well-crafted Artillery Bow will easily carry you to the endgame and early mapping.
*   **Ascendancy Order:** 
    1.  Point Blank (Proximity Specialization)
    2.  Endless Munitions
    3.  Gathering Winds
*   **Freezing Mark Quality:** Try to get a high-quality version of the Freezing Mark gem later on. Quality adds up to 20% more freeze buildup, making endgame bosses significantly easier to lock down for Snipes.
